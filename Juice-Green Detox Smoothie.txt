Juice: Green Detox Smoothie

Ingredients:
1 cup spinach
1/2 cucumber
1 green apple, cored
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 cup water or coconut water
Optional: 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup

Instructions:
1) Combine spinach, cucumber, apple, lemon juice, and water in a blender.
2) Blend until smooth.
3) Taste and add honey or agave syrup if desired for extra sweetness.
4) Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Time: About 5 minutes.
