Food: Veggie Wraps

Ingredients:
1 large tortilla
2 tablespoons hummus
1/2 avocado, sliced
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup baby spinach
1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional: feta cheese or grilled chicken

Instructions:
1) Spread hummus evenly over the tortilla.
2) Arrange avocado slices, shredded carrots, spinach, and cucumber in the center of the tortilla.
3) Add salt and pepper to taste.
4) Optionally, add feta cheese or grilled chicken if desired.
5) Roll the tortilla tightly, slice in half, and serve.

Time: About 5 minutes.
